# Audio Design

Audio is handled by `AudioSys` in `game/js/engine.js`.

Current audio categories:

- Music.
- Ambient loops.
- SFX.
- Tape noise.
- Heartbeat.

Prologue audio use:

- Bus and station: rain and wind.
- Old house: rain, house music, recorder tape noise.
- Cellar/tomb: drone and drip.
- Horror beats: paper, whisper, sob, stinger, flash/shake pairing.

Browser note:

Audio may be blocked until user interaction; all critical narrative text remains visible in UI.
