# Story Bible

Protagonist: Chen Guichuan, museum artifact restorer, returns to Huaiyin in 1998 after receiving a wet wooden box.

Opening objects:

- Half jade charm.
- Rusty key.
- Father tape.
- Soaked family photo.
- Old newspaper accusing Chen Shouyi of tomb robbery.

Prologue locations:

- Last bus: impossible passenger count, wooden box.
- Abandoned station: dead phone, stopped clock, road marker.
- Rainy mountain road: paper person and old stele.
- Chen old house: warm bowls, recorder, mother room, cellar hatch.
- Cellar / unnamed family tomb: tools, coffin, tape box, artisan name wall.

Emotional spine:

Chen Guichuan is not only looking for his mother. He is walking through all the places where his family chose silence instead of truth.
