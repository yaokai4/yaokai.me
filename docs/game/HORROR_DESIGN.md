# Horror Design

Horror sources:

- Darkness and weak lamp radius.
- Rain, wind, drone, drip, tape noise, sob, stingers.
- Single-use triggers.
- Props that change meaning after interaction.
- Sanity loss and visual wobble.

Implemented prologue beats:

- Last bus passenger count unease.
- Station phone with mother's voice.
- Rainy road paper person.
- Old-house warm bowls.
- Father tape on old recorder.
- Paper effigy appears after tape playback.
- Cellar coffin and artisan name wall.

Rule:

Horror events should reveal information, change a goal, or alter the player’s relationship to a place. Cheap jumpscares are secondary.
